FESTIVALS

Information about festivals

The below feature the festivals in the UK and in new ones in other countries that are hosted by us.

Isle of Wight Festival 2020

You surely have heard of Isle of Wight Festival. This among one of UK's longest running Festivals. This year's lineup will feature:

Lionel Richie, Lewis Capaldi, Snow Patrol, The Chemical Brothers and Duran.

The festival is also family friendly with a theatre, activities and games.

The Festival commences June 17-21 2020

Sundown Festival 2020

Sundown Festival is a UK bass and pop music festival, bringing some of the best artists in the scene to its home at the Norfolk Showground.

Sundown's 2020 lineup will be headlined by Sean Paul and Loyle Carner, with others coming up.

Sundown Festival also features a Funfair for visitors.

The Festival commences September 4th - 6th 2020.

Roskilde Festival 2020

Roskilde Fesival is a Danish festival located in Roskilde, Denmark. It was created in 1971 by two High School Students.

Roskilde 2020's lineup features many top global artists/bands. Such as: Taylor Swift, Tyler the Creator, The Strokes and many more.

Roskilde Festival features a camp where attendies can camp through the duration of the festival.

The festival commences June 28th - July 1st.

Glitch Malta 2020

Glitch Malta 2020 is a music festival taken place in Rabat, Malta. It is a festival dedicated to the Techno, House genres.

Glitch Malta 2020's lineup will feature Amelie Lens, Daniel Avery, Laurent Garnier and others

The festival features rooftop pool parties, secret rave caves, chaotic boat parties.

The festival commences August 14th - 16th 2020.
