Boston Calling 2020

Boston Calling music festival is a musical festival based in Boston, USA. This festival covers a variety of genres. From classic rock and roll to Pop Rock.

Boston calling will have a number of famous artists from around the globe playing, such as Red Hot chilli peppers, The 1975 and more!

Boston Calling is to be held on the 22nd May - 24th May in Boston, USA. Follow the link to the Booking Form to book a ticket.

Wireless Germany 2020

Wireless Germany is the German edition for London-born Wireless music festival. The festival will be based in Frankfurt am Main, Germany.

Wireless Germany will feature 3 genres but a vast majority of their artists. Hop, Rap and Pop. There is also an urban lifestyle playground for the family.

Wireless Germany is to be held on the 10th July - 11th July in Frankfurt am Main, Germany. Follow the link to the Booking Form to book a ticket.

Isle of Wight Festival 2020

Isle of Wight is an old running festival (since 1968). The festival will be based in NewPort, Isle of Wight.

Isle of Wight 4 types of genres. R&B, Rock, Pop, Indie. There will be an area for families with a theatre, activities and games.

Isle of Wight is to be held on the 17th June - 21st in NewPort, Isle of Wight. Follow the link to the Booking Form to book a ticket.
