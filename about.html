About NCC Music Festivals

Who are we?

NCC Music Festival was formed originally in Manchaster in 1995 by our parenting Company NCC Education. Our aim till 2009 was to provide Music Festivals all over the country with the satisfaction of artists/bands playing to their fans and fans being able to hear their "heroes" live.

Global Goal

In 2009, We have decided to continue expanding throughout the globe. Reaching Destinations such as Germany, Malta, Denmark, etc...
We have decided to expand throughout the globe to expand recognition of NCC Music Festival and allow more people to meet their favourite artists around the globe through our means. As how one of our visitors have said:

"Before NCC Music Festival started to host festivals in Malta, It wouldn't be possible to see Sabaton play live here.
Thanks to them I managed to see them live here for they came to their Summer 2018 event." - Anon.
